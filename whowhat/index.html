Meet Berlin Calling

Some of Us…

The group that makes up Berlin Calling or “the crew” as we like to ourselves are a collecilective of indivduals jumbled together from across the globe. Celebrating the melting pot genre of Techno, that has its origins stemmed from the depth of Berlin, as well as its various derivates that lie in the world of Electronic Dance Music is a true passion for us and to be able to impact others from even further places has been and will stay an incredible job!

Organising large opportunities for the people to dance and get tribal in the presence of the tropics is what we tend to do on the regular.
